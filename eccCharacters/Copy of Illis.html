Illis Illsder

Name: Illis Illsder
Gender: Genderfluid
DoB: 07/02/1995
PoB: Norway
Height: 170cm
Physical Description: Tall, light skin, short brown hair, average build
Likes: Peace, Justice, Cranberries
Dislikes: Small spaces, the color orange
Class: Lv 3 Telekinesis [move objects with mind], Lv 1 Tychokinesis [lucky]
Special Skills: Pokemonm Diplomany, Swimming, Swordplay, Speed
Languages: Norweigian, English
Personality traits: Fun-loving, kind, happy,
Organisation?: St Astrids Church, Norweigian government
Details: Came 1st in their class on modern swordfighting, their Tychokinesis lending them an edge over other compeditors. Despite this, they do not like physical combat preferring traditional negotiation methods instead. Married to Doctor Su-Ah Hallsder and working as a Peacekeeper for the Norweigian Government alongside a small group of Abberants tasks with conflict resolution. Often deployed alongside ambulance officials as their Telekinesis makes them of great assistance when moving injured patients. Spends much time assisting with running St Astrids Church, a relativley new church with the philosophies of showing kindness to others and standing strong in the face of hardship. Dreams of using their powers to help with greater, less stressful matters such as building repair or helping show younger Abberants how to use their powers (for good causes). Raised in a temporary housing camp from age 10 alongside her older sister Ingrid, neither them nor their father had it easy after fire destoryed their home, leaving them in homeless. When Illis was around 13 they moved out of the camp and into a small house on the outskirts of Oslo.
