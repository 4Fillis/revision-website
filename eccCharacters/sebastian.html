Señor Sebastian Spideymuffin
Small spider boy (self named)

Name: Señor Sebastian Spideymuffin
Gender: Male
DoB: 10/12/2011
PoB: Madrid, Spain
Location: Costa Brava, Spain
Height: 140cm
Physical Description: Small, shy, fluffy, spiderlike
Likes: Tea, biochem, spiders
Dislikes: Misinformation Class: Lv 3 Shapeshifter
Special Skills: climbing, hiding, swinging
Languages: Spanish, French
Personality traits: Shy, friendly, likes dancing
Organisation?: N/A
Details: Raised in a lower/mid class area in east Madrid by their older siblings and others after parents died in a bakery accident. At age 9 was present at the SNCB (Spanish National Center for Biotechnology) biochem leak possibly giving them an accelerated intellegience and/or other effects, though this is hard to gaugue given Spideymuffins age at the time. Shifted into their typical spiderlike form when 14 and lacking control of their powers, this form remains their preferred way of preseting. Attended high school with an interest in micobiology and social studies, pursued this at higher education, graduating in 2030. As of May 1st 2030 is part-time employed at TVE working under Sera Rojas as a journalist.
